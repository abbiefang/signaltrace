# Signal Trace — Logo Design Brief

## App Name

Signal Trace

## What This App Does (one sentence)

A mobile-first AI dating decision system that helps users turn messy dating signals into clear judgment and action.

## Logo Type

App icon only. Pure graphic mark. No text, no wordmark.

---

## Core Concept

The logo should visually express one of these ideas (pick the strongest one):

1. **From noise to clarity** — scattered, ambiguous signals resolving into one sharp, clear line or point
2. **Tracing a signal** — following a path through complexity to arrive at an answer
3. **Focused perception** — the moment something blurry becomes sharp

The logo should NOT literally depict:
- A heart or heartbeat
- A magnifying glass
- A chat bubble
- An eye
- A radar screen
- Letters or initials

---

## Visual Direction

### Style
- Geometric, not organic
- Minimal, not decorative
- Precise, not hand-drawn
- Abstract, not literal
- Modern tech aesthetic, but warm — not cold or clinical

### Shape Language
- Clean lines, smooth curves
- Favor circles, arcs, and flowing strokes over sharp angles
- One focal point — the icon should have a single visual anchor
- Negative space is encouraged

### Complexity
- Should read clearly at 32x32 px (app icon on phone)
- Maximum 2-3 visual elements
- If it needs more than 3 seconds to "get it," it's too complex

---

## Color Direction

### Primary palette (choose one approach)

**Option A — Dark background**
- Background: deep navy or near-black (#12101c to #1a1a2e)
- Mark: warm gradient — coral to soft pink (#e94560 → #f093fb)
- Accent: a single bright point of light

**Option B — Light background**
- Background: warm off-white (#f8f7fc to #faf8fc)
- Mark: muted rose or warm coral (#e94560)
- Accent: soft purple (#6c5ce7) as secondary

### Color rules
- No more than 2 colors in the mark itself
- Gradient is OK if subtle and directional
- Avoid pure black strokes
- Avoid neon / saturated blue / tech-blue
- The overall feel should be: warm clarity, not cold precision

---

## Emotional Tone

The logo should feel:
- Clear
- Grounded
- Intelligent
- Calm confidence
- Slightly premium

The logo should NOT feel:
- Playful / bubbly
- Aggressive / edgy
- Corporate / enterprise
- Cute / feminine-stereotypical
- Cold / clinical

---

## Target Audience

- Women 25-40
- Intentional daters in large cities
- Design-aware, iPhone users
- Value clarity and self-respect over trends

---

## Comparable Design References (for mood, not copying)

- Calm app icon (simple gradient, one focused shape)
- Linear app icon (clean geometry, premium feel)
- Arc browser icon (modern, warm gradient, distinctive shape)
- Notion icon (extreme simplicity, recognizable at any size)
- Headspace icon (warm, approachable, not cold)

---

## What Makes a Good Signal Trace Icon

It should pass these tests:

1. **Thumbnail test**: Recognizable at 32px on a phone home screen
2. **Instant read**: The shape registers in under 2 seconds
3. **No explanation needed**: You don't need to know the app name to feel "clarity" or "signal"
4. **Not generic**: It shouldn't look like every other wellness / dating / productivity app
5. **Ages well**: No trendy effects that will look dated in 2 years

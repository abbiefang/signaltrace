What this is

Not a feelings journal.

Signal Trace is a behavioral tracking system. It records what he actually does — not what you hope or fear — and surfaces patterns over time.

Not a diary for venting

How it works

Three steps. Ongoing clarity.

Signal Trace runs quietly in the background of your dating decisions. Here's the loop:

1

You log what actually happened

His exact words. What he did or didn't do. When. No editorializing — just the facts as you observed them.

2

AI reads patterns across time

Does his behavior match what he says? Is follow-through consistent? Are signals contradicting each other? The system surfaces what individual moments can miss.

3

You decide from a clearer position

With an evidence trail instead of impressions, your decisions get sharper — and harder to second-guess later.

💡 Signal Trace also lets you set exit criteria in advance — conditions you define before emotions cloud the picture.

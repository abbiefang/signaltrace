### Long Description

---

You already notice when something's off. You track the gap between what he says and what he does. You replay the timeline, weigh the evidence, and still can't make a clean call.

Signal Trace is built for that exact problem.

It's not a journal. It's not a relationship diary. It's a structured decision system — one that takes everything you're already tracking in your head and turns it into something you can actually use.

---

**For every person you're dating, Signal Trace gives you:**

A running evidence log — what he said, what he did, and how long the gap was between them.

A conflict detector that surfaces contradictions explicitly. Not averaged out. Not buried. Side by side, with timestamps: what he said he wanted, and what he's actually done.

AI behavioral predictions — based on his pattern, what's most likely to happen next, what it means if it does, and what it means if it doesn't.

A pre-mortem: before you're attached, you set your own exit criteria. If he cancels twice, if he never proposes a concrete plan, if his words and actions stay inconsistent — you decide. When those conditions are met, the app reminds you. Not AI telling you what to do. You, from two weeks ago, reminding yourself.

A decision journal that tracks every call you've made and why — so you can see your own reasoning over time, not just your feelings in the moment.

A compatibility layer that shows where you actually match, where you don't, and what gaps matter versus which ones are noise.

---

**The dashboard tracks your whole pipeline** — not just individual people, but the system you're running. When you're managing too many at once, it tells you. When you've been postponing the same decision across three people for two weeks, it names that too.

---

**When it works, it works differently.**

If someone earns it, Signal Trace doesn't just help you eliminate people — it helps you commit with clarity. The Committed state turns your decision page into an anchor: here is why this person made sense, based on evidence, across enough time to trust.

You can come back to it in six months when things feel uncertain. It will still say the same thing. Because you wrote it when you were clear-headed.

---

**What Signal Trace is not:**

It is not a hot-or-not ranker. It does not tell you whether to text him back. It does not generate openers, suggest date ideas, or tell you he's wrong for you. It does not reward obsessing.

It is a private, structured record of your own judgment — designed to get sharper over time, not more anxious.

---

**Built for women who date intentionally.** Who have standards they've thought about. Who don't want to be talked out of what they noticed. Who want to make decisions they can explain to themselves, not just feel good about in the moment.

Signal Trace gives your judgment a structure it deserves.

## Competitive Differentiation Analysis

### The landscape as it actually is

There is no direct competitor to Signal Trace. That is both the opportunity and the challenge. Most women currently handle this problem with a combination of: texting friends, keeping screenshots in a folder, typing notes in their phone's Notes app, maintaining a Notion page, or nothing — just holding it all in their head.

The "dating tracker" category in the App Store is sparse and mostly poor. What exists falls into four buckets, all with meaningful limitations:

---

**1. Dating journals (Day One, Reflectly, generic diary apps)**

The ceiling: unstructured reflection with no analytical layer. Users can write anything, but the app cannot surface patterns, flag inconsistencies, or prompt structured judgment. The output is a record, not a decision tool. Writing about how he made you feel is not the same as analyzing whether his behavior is consistent with what he says he wants.

Signal Trace difference: structured evidence capture, AI pattern analysis, conflict detection. The journal is the input, not the product.

---

**2. Spreadsheet-style date trackers (manual, Notion templates, DIY)**

The ceiling: maximum structure, zero intelligence. You can log everything in a spreadsheet, but the spreadsheet won't tell you that he's proposed zero concrete plans in four weeks, or that your last three "Continue" decisions were based on texts rather than in-person behavior. The friction of setup also means most people abandon it within two weeks.

Signal Trace difference: opinionated structure built in, AI layer that reads what you log and tells you what it means. Low-friction capture (Quick Capture, voice debrief), high-output intelligence.

---

**3. Dating advice / coaching apps**

The ceiling: generic advice that doesn't know your specific situation. An app that tells you "he should be texting first" doesn't know that he's been planning every date himself for six weeks but goes quiet between them — and whether that means something or nothing requires knowing the rest of the pattern.

Signal Trace difference: no advice, only analysis of your specific data. The app doesn't tell you what to want. It helps you see clearly what you're already looking at.

---

**4. Matching apps (Hinge, Bumble, Tinder, Hinge)**

Not competitors in function, but the context in which Signal Trace operates. These apps are optimized for volume and initial attraction. They have no mechanism for helping you evaluate what happens after the match — which is where most of the ambiguity and emotional cost lives.

Signal Trace difference: picks up exactly where matching apps leave off. Not about meeting people. About deciding on them.

---

### The actual whitespace Signal Trace owns

No current app addresses: the gap between "I matched with someone and we've been dating for three weeks" and "I know whether to keep investing." That gap is where most emotional cost accumulates — and it's exactly what Signal Trace is built for.

The three things that set it apart from anything existing:

**Conflicting signal detection** — explicitly surfacing contradictions rather than averaging them. No other tool does this.

**Pre-mortem exit criteria** — letting the user set her standards before she's attached and be reminded of them when they're met. Not AI judgment. Self-accountability with structural support.

**Evidence-based committed state** — a positive outcome track. Signal Trace is not just an elimination machine. When someone earns it, the product helps the user document why, so she has something to return to when doubt shows up later.

Design Language
Reference

A complete visual system for Signal Trace — an AI dating decision tool for women who are discerning, self-aware, and done with ambiguity. Tone: intelligent, warm, trustworthy. Never clinical, never cute.

11 — Visual Tone

Copy & Visual Tone

The visual system and the copy are inseparable. The AI voice should sound like the most clear-headed version of the user — not a therapist, not a cheerleader, not a warning label.

Use
Your pre-set exit criteria has been met. This is you, from two weeks ago, reminding yourself.
You are tracking 6 active people. Your best decisions usually happen with 3–4.
His words say interest. His actions say delay. The conflict is between stated intent and actual follow-through.
You did not commit out of excitement. You committed because the evidence stayed healthy.
You have postponed 3 decisions this week. This may not be about them.
Avoid
You're burning out! Take a break NOW.
He's clearly lying to you.
You should definitely commit to this person.
You're doing amazing! Keep going! 🌟
Based on our analysis, it seems like your emotional state may be affecting your judgment at this time.
Tone Attributes
Clear
Every piece of text has one job. No hedging. No filler. If you're uncertain, say so directly — don't soften with vague language.
Grounded
AI statements cite evidence. Recommendations name the specific pattern driving them. The user can always trace why the AI said what it said.
Calm confidence
No exclamation marks. No emoji in functional UI. No urgency language unless the situation genuinely requires it. The product doesn't escalate the user's anxiety.
Precise
Named dates, specific counts, real quotes. "He texted 12 hours later" not "he followed up quickly." Specificity is the product's core value.
Non-directive
The AI presents evidence and analysis. It does not tell the user what to feel or what to do. The user is always in control. The product is a tool, not a verdict.
Slightly premium
Restrained type. Generous white space. No rounded-bubbly UI. This is a decision tool, not a social app. The visual restraint signals that the product takes the user seriously.
